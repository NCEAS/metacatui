People

For each person, provide at least: a person's first and last name, or a position name, or an organization name.

Creators

Each person or organization listed as a Creator will be listed in the data citation. At least one person, organization, or position with a 'Creator' role is required.

If a creator is not provided, you will be set as the creator.

Contacts

If a contact is not provided, the first creator's information will be used for the contact.
